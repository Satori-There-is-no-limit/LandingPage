What's Satori?

Satori is not only a team, it's a full community of people who love to use, create and share their knowledge and software.


Creative

Our community and staff are very creative, and we are always looking for new ideas. Since they are working hand in hand together, they are able to create amazing things.

Open Source

We are developing your ideas, but we are not doing it alone. The whole team and even the community is working on it. After that, everything will be released for free on GitHub, OpenSource.

Clever

We have 8 developers in our team. Every one of them has his own expirience and knowledge. This is why satori is able to create amazing stuff which is stable and secure.


Non-Profit

We are not a company, we are a community. We are not here to make money, we are here to make your ideas come true and to improve our programming skills.

Friendly

Our community is very friendly and helpful. We are always here to help you with your problems, no matter if it is a programming problem or just a problem with your grandmas printer.

Adaptable

We will never say something like "We can't do that". Okay, if your idea is malware, we'll say that. But otherwhise, we will try to make it happen. We are always looking for new challenges.
